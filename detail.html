The Kerala Story box office collection Day 3: Sudipto Sen’s film soon to join Rs 40 crore club

5/8/2023

The Kerala Story box office collection Day 3: Since the film opened in theatres this week, The Kerala Story, directed by Sudipto Sen, has seen an increase in ticket sales. On Sunday, May 7, the movie made twice as much money as it did on Day 1. However, several theatres in Tamil Nadu and numerous other states decided to cancel the film’s showing. Madhya Pradesh announced The Kerala Story to be tax-free. Box Office Collection On May 5, The film entered theatres. Both crowds and critics have given it varying ratings. The movie made Rs 8.03 crore (net) on its first day of release in India. On Sunday, May 7, the movie’s estimated domestic box office gross was Rs 16.50 crore (nett). Compared to Day 1, this collection is twice as large. The Kerala Story has already earned a total of Rs 35.75 crore in India.
